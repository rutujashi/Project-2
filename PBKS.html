Punjab Kings

INFORMATION OF PBKS

Punjab Kings (PBKS) is a professional franchise cricket team that represents the state of Punjab in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and has been part of the tournament since its inaugural season in 2008, earlier known as Kings XI Punjab before being rebranded as Punjab Kings in 2021. The team plays its home matches at the IS Bindra Stadium in Mohali, which is known for its lively pitch and enthusiastic crowd support. PBKS is jointly owned by Preity Zinta, Ness Wadia, Mohit Burman, and Karan Paul, making it one of the most well-known franchises in terms of celebrity ownership. Over the years, Punjab Kings have been recognized for their aggressive playing style, powerful batting lineups, and willingness to back young and emerging talent. Although the team has reached the IPL final once in 2014, they have often faced challenges in maintaining consistency across seasons. Despite this, PBKS continues to be a competitive and entertaining side, frequently producing thrilling matches and standout individual performances. The franchise enjoys a loyal fan base, especially in Punjab and northern India, due to its fighting spirit and never-give-up attitude. In IPL 2026, Punjab Kings continue their efforts to build a balanced and strong team, aiming for consistent performances and playoff success while contributing exciting cricket and entertainment to the Indian Premier League.
