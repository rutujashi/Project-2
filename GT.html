Gujarat Titans

INFORMATION OF GT

Gujarat Titans (GT) is a professional franchise cricket team that represents the state of Gujarat in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and is one of the newer teams in the tournament, having made its debut in IPL 2022. The team is owned by CVC Capital Partners and plays its home matches at the iconic Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, the largest cricket stadium in the world, known for hosting high-profile national and international matches. Gujarat Titans created history by delivering an outstanding performance in their debut season by winning the IPL 2022 title, which instantly established them as a strong and well-organized franchise. GT is known for its calm leadership, disciplined bowling attack, and efficient team strategy, focusing on teamwork rather than individual brilliance alone. The franchise has quickly built a strong fan base across Gujarat and neighboring regions due to its consistent performances and professional management. Over the seasons, Gujarat Titans have continued to be a competitive side, regularly challenging top teams and qualifying for the playoffs. In IPL 2026, Gujarat Titans remain an important and exciting team in the league, bringing stability, confidence, and high-quality cricket to the tournament while continuing to enhance the popularity of the IPL among fans across India and around the world.
