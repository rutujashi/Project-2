Delhi Capitals

INFORMATION OF DC

Delhi Capitals (DC) is a professional franchise cricket team that represents Delhi, the capital city of India, in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and has been part of the tournament since its inception in 2008, earlier known as Delhi Daredevils before being rebranded as Delhi Capitals in 2019. The team plays its home matches at the Arun Jaitley Stadium in Delhi, a historic venue known for its passionate crowd and lively atmosphere. Delhi Capitals is jointly owned by the GMR Group and JSW Group, and the franchise is well known for its strong focus on developing young Indian talent alongside experienced international players. Over the years, DC has built a reputation for playing fearless and aggressive cricket, especially in recent seasons where the team has shown consistent performances and reached the playoffs multiple times, including finishing as runners-up in IPL 2020. The franchise has produced and supported several future stars of Indian cricket, emphasizing fitness, teamwork, and modern playing strategies. With a balanced combination of explosive batting, effective bowling, and dynamic fielding, Delhi Capitals have become a competitive force in the league. In IPL 2026, Delhi Capitals continue to aim for their first IPL title, bringing energy, ambition, and exciting cricket to the tournament while maintaining a strong connection with their fans across the country.
