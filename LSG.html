Lucknow Super Giants

INFORMATION OF LSG

Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) is a professional franchise cricket team that represents Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and has quickly established itself as a strong and competitive side since its debut in IPL 2022. The team is owned by the RPSG Group, led by industrialist Sanjiv Goenka, and plays its home matches at the BRSABV Ekana Cricket Stadium in Lucknow, which is one of the modern stadiums in India and is known for hosting high-quality international and domestic matches. LSG is recognized for its well-balanced squad, combining experienced international players with talented Indian cricketers, which has helped the team perform consistently in the league. From its very first season, Lucknow Super Giants impressed cricket fans by qualifying for the playoffs, showing strong leadership, disciplined bowling, and aggressive yet controlled batting performances. The franchise has built a loyal fan base in a short period of time, especially in Uttar Pradesh and nearby regions, due to its fighting spirit and professional management. In IPL 2026, Lucknow Super Giants continue to be an important and exciting team in the tournament, bringing fresh energy, competitive intensity, and entertaining cricket to the league, and contributing to the overall popularity and success of the Indian Premier League across India and the world.
