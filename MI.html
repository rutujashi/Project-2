INFORMATION OF MI

Mumbai Indians (MI) is one of the most successful and popular franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL), representing the city of Mumbai, Maharashtra. The team is owned by Indiawin Sports, a subsidiary of Reliance Industries, and was founded in 2008 when the IPL began. Mumbai Indians play their home matches at the iconic Wankhede Stadium, which is known for its electrifying atmosphere and passionate fans. The team is famous for its strong core, excellent scouting system, and ability to groom young talent into match-winners. Mumbai Indians have won the IPL title five times (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020), making them one of the most successful teams in league history. Over the years, MI has been led by legendary players like Sachin Tendulkar and Rohit Sharma, with Rohit being the most successful captain in IPL history.
